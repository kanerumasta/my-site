## Hello World

Hi, my name is Mac. I’m a software engineer who also loves playing the guitar.
This blog is my space to document what I learn as I build projects and explore new technologies.

## Why I Started This Blog

While working on different projects, I realized that I often forget the things I’ve already learned.
Sometimes I solve a problem, and a few weeks later—I have to figure it out again.

So I decided to start writing.

This blog will help me:

* remember what I’ve learned
* improve how I explain things
* track my growth as a developer

## What I’ll Be Sharing

Most of my posts will be simple and practical:

* bugs I encountered and how I fixed them
* small discoveries while coding
* lessons from real projects
* things I wish I knew earlier

Nothing too fancy—just real experiences.

## My Approach

I won’t wait until I’m an “expert” to write.

Instead, I’ll document:

* what I’m learning
* what confuses me
* and how I solve problems

Because that’s where the real value is.

## Final Thoughts

This is just the beginning.

I’m building, learning, and documenting—one step at a time.

Let’s see where this goes 🚀
